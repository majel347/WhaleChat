Indian Whaling:A Conversation Between Ramona Peters & Jason Mancini

Whale Chat draws from the original recording of a conversation between Ramona Peters and John Mancini. The conversation took place as the two collaborated on an exhibit at the Mashpee Wampanoag Indian Museum showcasing Mashpee Whalers, and showcases their different interests and perspectives on the topic.
